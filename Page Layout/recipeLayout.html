Buttery Grits

savory, creamy and warm

  • 2 cups water
  • 1/4 cup grits
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  1. Bring 2 cups water with salt to a boil
  2. Slowly add grits while stirring consistently
  3. Cover and let boil on medium low for 15 mins
  4. Add butter and 1/4 water. Stir vigorously. Then let boil for 10-15mins more
  5. Turn on low and let simmer for 5 mins
  6. Remove from heat. Stir vigorously. Let sit for 5mins

This is a great recipe for savory dishes. It pairs great with any side dish from your your recipe book
